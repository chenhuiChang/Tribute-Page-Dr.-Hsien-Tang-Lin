Dr. Hsien-Tang Lin
The man who made great efforts to parliament for Taiwan

Dr. Hsien-Tang Lin's picture

Here's a time line of Dr. Lin's life:

  • 1881 - Born in Attabu(Wufeng District), Taiwan.
  • 1895 - Family left for Chaozhou in China bacause of Treaty of Shimonoseki. After years, came back to Taiwan.
  • 1902 - Became the mayor of Dongshi.
  • 1907 - Liang Qichao adviced Lin to do nonviolence action for the self-governance of Taiwan.
  • 1913 - Lin and his friends found Taichung First Senior High School, the first school for Taiwan native children.
  • 1920 - Became the leader of New People Society, the first organization for political movement, created by Taiwanese students in Japan.
  • 1921 - Send letter of establishment of a Taiwanese Parliament to Japan Reichstag.
  • 1921 - Found Taiwanese Cultural Association with Chiang Wei-shui.
  • 1927 - Found Taiwanese People's Party.
  • 1937 - China and Japan had war. Parties dismissed.
  • 1947 - Because of the February 28 Incident, Chinese government made Lin very disappointed.
  • 1956 - Dies at the age of 74.

    • "The leader and caregiver of the movement of establishment of self-governance in Taiwan."
